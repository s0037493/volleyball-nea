The direction of passes means they will always go to your teammate
Sets are almost the same as passing, but the direction is manual.
This means, if you want your teammate to set from a specific place, use a set instead of a pass.


If you are close enough to your teammate when they set, they will set you a quick attack.


A jump serve will have more power than a normal serve


You can jump and hit to block your opponent, but it will be less powerful than a normal hit
